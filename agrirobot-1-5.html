AgriRobot — Part guide

  • Roof / solar panels — powers the robot; batteries and control electronics sit underneath inside the roof.
  • Main body — internal storage room for water and seed reserves.
  • Front sensor — ultrasonic "eyes" that watch the ground ahead and stop the robot before it drives into an obstacle.
  • Drill units — two augers that each bore their own planting hole.
  • Grass cutter — green struts hold a row of spikes that mow grass under the front, used only when the ground is grassy (toggle it off if not needed).
  • Sprinklers — two rear nozzles that water the planted spot.
  • Dirt coverer — rear panel that folds down to cover the seed.
  • Seed outlets — four droppers in a 2×2 grid; each left/right pair feeds seed into its matching hole.

Planting order (front to back, matching the real layout): cutter → drills → seed outlets → cover flap → sprinklers. In Continuous Plant Mode the robot never stops — each station works on the ground as it passes underneath, just like a real seeder.
